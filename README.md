## ğŸ§  Interview Questions (Short Answers)

### 1. What is phishing?
A cyber-attack tricking users into giving sensitive information.

### 2. How to identify a phishing email?
Check sender address, links, grammar, tone, and attachments.

### 3. What is email spoofing?
Faking the sender's identity using a forged email address.

### 4. Why are phishing emails dangerous?
They steal credentials, money, install malware, etc.

### 5. How to verify sender authenticity?
Check the real domain, SPF/DKIM/DMARC, hover links.

### 6. Tools for email header analysis?
MXToolbox, Google Admin Toolbox, Microsoft Analyzer.

### 7. What to do with a suspected phishing email?
Do NOT click anything; report and delete it.

### 8. How do attackers use social engineering?
Through urgency, fear, threats, fake rewards, trust exploitation.
